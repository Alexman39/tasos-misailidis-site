What You Get

The 5-Day Hypertrophy Program is built for lifters who want a structured, intense, and repeatable plan for maximum muscle growth. Every training session is designed to hit each muscle group with the frequency and volume needed for real progress — without junk volume or guesswork.

  • Complete 5-day hypertrophy split (Push • Pull • Legs • Upper • Arms/Shoulders)
  • 10-week structured progression model, repeatable for long-term gains
  • Warm-up, activation, and basic mobility guidelines
  • Exercise selection optimized for hypertrophy and joint safety
  • RPE and progression instructions so every set has a clear purpose
  • Tips for recovery, intensity techniques, and sustaining progress over time

Is This Program For You?

This program is a great fit if you:

  • Want to build muscle, strength, and a balanced aesthetic physique
  • Prefer a clear, structured plan instead of guessing your workouts
  • Enjoy training with higher frequency (each muscle group 2–3× per week)

  • Want a repeatable hypertrophy system you can run multiple times per year
