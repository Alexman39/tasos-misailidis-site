What You Get

The Competition Prep Program lays out your full training structure for the final 16 weeks leading into a show. It’s designed as a training backbone around which you can plug your nutrition strategy.

  • 16-week training plan focused on retaining muscle while dieting hard
  • High frequency for key muscle groups and weak points

  • General peak week training guidelines

Is This Program For You?

  • You are preparing for a bodybuilding or physique competition
  • You already have a solid training base and experience
  • You can commit to 5–6 sessions per week consistently
  • You understand or will work with someone on the nutrition side
