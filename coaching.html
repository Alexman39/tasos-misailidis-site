How Online Coaching Works

1. Assessment

You share your training history, lifestyle, current condition, injuries and goal (fat loss, muscle gain, recomposition, competition prep).

2. Custom Plan

Tasos designs a personalized training plan and nutrition guidelines matched to your experience, equipment and available days per week.

3. Check-ins & Adjustments

You send weekly updates, photos and feedback. Based on your progress, the plan is adjusted so you keep moving forward.

What You Get with Online Coaching
